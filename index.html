FAQ

Common questions from customers, technicians, and companies.

How do I choose the right technician?

Compare ratings, reviews, experience, and pricing. You can also filter by category and location.

Is pricing fixed?

Easy Fix aims for transparent pricing. Providers can display price lists, and booking requests can include service details for accurate estimates.

How does the women-for-women option work?

During booking, female customers can request female technicians (where available). This option is shown clearly to support comfort and safety.

How can technicians get more jobs?

Complete your profile, keep your prices clear, and maintain strong ratings through quality service and good communication.
